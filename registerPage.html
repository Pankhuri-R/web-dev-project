Help Guide

This is a guide to help you fill the form.

  • First you need to fill your full name,it should be same as the name in your identity proof.
  • Second is a valid email id. You'll recieve all related emails there.
  • Next is your current phone number.Kindly write it in the given format only.
  • From the drop down menu choose the course you want to apply for.
  • Next comes through which quota you're willing to take admission.Make your choice carefully.
  • You also need to choose whether you're 12th appearing or passed.
  • Click on the register button after you're done with all the sections. Also cross check before clicking register.

NOTE: Except your phone number all other sections are compulsory to fill. Carefully fill all the details as you won't be able to edit anything after you submit.
